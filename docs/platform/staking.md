## ğŸ”“ Unstaking

### How to Unstake
1. Go to Staking page
2. Click **"Unstake"** tab
3. Enter amount to withdraw
4. Click **"Unstake"**
5. Confirm in wallet
6. OGUN returns to your wallet

### Considerations
- No lock period (flexible unstaking)
- Gas fees apply
- Unclaimed rewards are auto-claimed on unstake

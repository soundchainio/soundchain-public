## Upload Process

### Step 1: Create Your Track

1. Click **"Create"** in the navigation
2. Upload your audio file (MP3, WAV, FLAC, M4A)
3. Add cover artwork (recommended: 1400x1400)
4. Fill in track details:
   - Title
   - Description
   - Genre
   - Tags

## Pinata IPFS Storage

All SoundChain tracks are stored on **Pinata IPFS** - a permanent, decentralized storage network.

**Benefits:**
- **Permanent** - Your music lives forever
- **Decentralized** - Not controlled by any single company
- **Fast** - Global CDN for quick playback
- **Verifiable** - Anyone can verify authenticity

## FAQ

<details>
<summary><strong>Do I need a wallet to upload?</strong></summary>

No! You can upload stream-only tracks without a wallet. You'll only need a wallet to:
- Mint as NFT
- Claim your OGUN rewards
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Can I convert a stream-only track to NFT later?</strong></summary>

Yes! You can mint any of your existing tracks as NFTs at any time.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Is my music really permanent on IPFS?</strong></summary>

Yes! Pinata pins your content to IPFS permanently. Even if SoundChain disappeared, your music would still exist on IPFS.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>What file formats are supported?</strong></summary>

- MP3 (recommended)
- WAV
- FLAC
- M4A
- OGG
</details>

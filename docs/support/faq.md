## Streaming Rewards

<details>
<summary><strong>How do WIN-WIN rewards work?</strong></summary>

Every stream generates OGUN for both parties:
- **Creator** receives 70% (0.35 OGUN for NFT tracks)
- **Listener** receives 30% (0.15 OGUN for NFT tracks)

Streams must be 30+ seconds to count.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Why do NFT tracks earn more?</strong></summary>

NFT tracks earn 10x more (0.50 vs 0.05 OGUN) because:
- NFTs represent premium content
- Incentivizes collectors and creators
- Rewards verified ownership
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>How do I claim my rewards?</strong></summary>

1. Tap the **PiggyBank** icon
2. View your earnings
3. Click **"Claim"**
4. Approve transaction in wallet
5. OGUN sent to your wallet!
</details>

## NFTs & Marketplace

<details>
<summary><strong>How do I mint an NFT?</strong></summary>

1. Upload your track
2. Add artwork and details
3. Select **"Mint as NFT"**
4. Set price and royalty %
5. Pay minting fee
6. Your NFT is live!
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>What are royalties?</strong></summary>

Creators can set up to 10% royalty on their NFTs. Every time the NFT is resold, you earn that percentage automatically.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>What happens to fees?</strong></summary>

- **0.05%** ‚Üí Treasury (funds streaming rewards)
- **2.5%** ‚Üí Platform operations
- **Up to 10%** ‚Üí Creator royalties
- **Rest** ‚Üí Seller
</details>
